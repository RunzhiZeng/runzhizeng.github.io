I am currently a post-doc researcher at Universität Kassel working with Jiaxin Pan. In September 2024, I completed my PhD at Norges Teknisk-Naturvitenskapelige Universitet under the supervision of Jiaxin Pan, with Tibor Jager as the co-examiner. My research interest is in the area of public-key cryptography, with emphasis on the following topics:

  • Design and analysis of authenticated key exchange (AKE) and password-based AKE

  • Construction of efficient lattice-based cryptographic schemes

  • Security analysis of post-quantum cryptosystems in quantum idealized models
